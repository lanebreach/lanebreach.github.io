Lane Breach Support

Effective date: March 01, 2019

iOS app

Lane Breach's iOS app makes it easy to report bike lane blockages to San Francisco's 311 service: just take a picture, select a category (Uber, bus, etc), enter an optional description and press SEND.

If you supply your email address, your lane blockage reports should also show up in the 311 app. Providing your name/phone number allows 311 to contact you if they have any questions. All fields are entirely optional.
